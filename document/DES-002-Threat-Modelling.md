| ID            |

## Summary

 Threat Modelling is a process of analyzing threats that can potentially impact the security of an application before it's deployed. 
 
 It helps to identify potential risks, the security controls needed to protect the application, and any potential mitigation strategies. 
 
 Threat Modelling is an important part of DevSecOps because it ensures that security considerations are taken into account throughout the entire development process, enabling organizations to build applications in a secure way from the start and minimize the risk of exploitation.
